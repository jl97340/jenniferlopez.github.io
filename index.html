Behind the Name

Welcome to my first blog post!

I am so excited to share my new platform where I can share my thoughts and inspiration daily. As part of my transition into the blogging world, I thought it would be nice to share my inspiration for the title of my blog "Espiritu Libre." The name itself is a Spanish translation meaning "free-spirit", and that is exactly why I chose to name it that! I am very much impacted daily by this saying, whether it be through my fashion style or my personality. I have an easy going, peaceful mindset and I love to show it physically and mentally in my life! I also love showing both diverse sides of my culture through my fashion choices, taking inspiration from vibrant, fun driven Mexican styles and also the bohemian, country look I have grown accustomed to seeing in South GA. I hope I am able to influence other young, diverse "free-spirits" out there find a little encouragement to be their best selves in the world!

What I'm Loving at the Moment

  • Gold Dainty Chokers:
  • The simplicity of these jewelry pieces adds a delicate statement to any outfit. Being a freshman college student, I have unwittedly realized that casual, comfortable outfits are the way to go from now on during school. The color of these pieces can help anyone feel a little more chic in their casual outfits!
  • Oversized Jean Jackets:
  • Going along with the theme of comfort and fashionable outfits, the classic jean jacket makes any outfit instantly become a new look. It's also breathable enough to wear during these upcoming warm Spring days!
  • Printed Tshirts:
  • Do I really have to emphasize the comfortable theme I have going in my life right now? Tshirts have definitely always been a staple in my closet, but I could never bring myself to style them in a fashion-forward way until I discovered the endless possibilities of wearing them! Wearing them with a jacket over top or even tying it a different way can add some flare into your everyday look.
